<p>The <b>Integumentary System</b> is the system of the body made up of the skin, the nails, and the hair.
</p><p>Skin keeps you cool by sweating. It also protects you by keeping things out of your body. The hair on your body keeps you warm.
Skin also holds <a href="/wiki/Wikijunior:Biology/Systems/Nervous_System" title="Wikijunior:Biology/Systems/Nervous System">nerves</a> that we use to touch and hold and kiss.  Your nails help you pick up things.  The skin is the largest organ of the human body.
