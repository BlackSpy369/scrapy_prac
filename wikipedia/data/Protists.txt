<p>Most <b>protists</b> are made of single cells.  They are bigger than <a href="/wiki/Wikijunior:Biology/Kingdoms/Archaea" title="Wikijunior:Biology/Kingdoms/Archaea">archaea</a> and <a href="/wiki/Wikijunior:Biology/Kingdoms/Bacteria" title="Wikijunior:Biology/Kingdoms/Bacteria">bacteria</a>, and protist cells have a nucleus.
</p><p>There are many different kinds of protist cells.  Most live in the water, but some live in the soil or in animals.  Some protists can cause diseases.
</p><p>Green Algae is in the kingdom Protista.  These can be small single cells or very large with many cells.  <i>Sea weed</i> is algae and is in the kingdom Protista.
