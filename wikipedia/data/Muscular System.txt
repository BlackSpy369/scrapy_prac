<p>The <i>muscles</i> of the body are what make the body move. 
They are made of muscle <a href="/wiki/Wikijunior:Biology/Tissues" title="Wikijunior:Biology/Tissues">tissues</a>.
</p><p>All of the muscles of the body together make up the <b>muscular system</b>. 
</p><p>Signals from the <a href="/wiki/Wikijunior:Biology/Systems/Nervous_System#Senses" title="Wikijunior:Biology/Systems/Nervous System">nervous system</a> tell the muscles when to move.  The muscles are attached to the <a href="/wiki/Wikijunior:Biology/Systems/Skeletal_System" title="Wikijunior:Biology/Systems/Skeletal System">skeleton</a> which holds them up.
